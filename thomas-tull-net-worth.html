American entrepreneur Thomas Tull has an estimated net worth of $550 million in 2012. He is best recognized as the founder of the Barbunk, California-based Legendary Pictures, Inc. where he also serves as the chief executive officer. Legendary Pictures Inc. is considered as one of the earliest companies that have raised its capital through hedge fund backing and private equity agreements. Some of the company's investors include AIG Direct Investments, Columbia Capital, Bank of America Capital Investors,  ABRY Partners, M/C Venture Partners and Falcon Investment Advisors.

Launched in 2005, the company specializes in creating, producing, and distributing major motion pictures. Some of the most successful films which the company has produced includes Batman Begins, and its sequels, The Dark Knight Rises, and The Dark Knight Returns. It also produced films titled  The Hangover, Watchmen, Superman Returns, The Town, Inception, Wrath of the Titans, and Clash of the Titans.

In addition to Legendary Pictures Inc., Thomas Tull is also a part owner of the Pittsburgh Steelers.
