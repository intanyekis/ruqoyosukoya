A Peek Inside Kristen Stewart's Car Collection

Kristen Stewart has garnered a whopping $70 million fortune through her prolific acting career, and the actress likes to spend her net worth buying classic and fast cars for her collection.

Coming from a background of parents who work in the show business, Kristen Stewart grew up on movie sets from a young age. Her first prominent performance occurred in 2002 when she starred in the mystery thriller Panic Room opposite Jodie Foster. Stewart starred in various films before bagging her breakthrough role in The Twilight Saga in 2008, playing the character of Isabella Swan; Kristen Stewart’s career skyrocketed to new heights. Since the series ended in 2012, Stewart has made careful choices working in independent movies with acclaimed directors that have helped her gain a $70 million net worth. The actress likes to spend her fortune primarily on real estate and her car collection.

Kristen Stewart is known to drive around Los Angeles in her pick-up truck, but she is also the owner of several European supercars worth a hundred thousand dollars. From Toyota trucks to a stunning Porsche, let’s take a look at her diverse car collection.

Mini Cooper S ($23,100)

A smooth and easy drive, Kristen Stewart has owned the Mini Cooper S since 2012. With a sporty, fast exterior, the Cooper S has a unique shape that catches everyone’s eye. As noted by Cars, The car is equipped with a 1.6-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine that gives a power output of 181 HP. The S coupe offers a $500 added sports suspension that is not seen in a base model Mini Cooper. The retail price of this stunning car is $23,100. The Cooper is not a family-friendly vehicle; however, it is an ideal vehicle for a couple’s weekend getaway or day-to-day activities with its added cargo space.

BMW X3 ($46,800)

Costing a cool $46,800, the actress has owned a BMW X3 for more than five years. A combination of potent performance and stunning interior, the X3 was ranked on the top spot in the 2016 luxury compact SUVs category. This model is a part of the 2011-2017 X3 series and is manufactured in three trims. The car features a 2.0-liter I-4 engine and is offered in the all-wheel and rear-wheel drivetrain that cranks out between 180-300 HP. The five-seater model has a 5.6-inch infotainment display and standard interior features seen in a BMW car.

Porsche 911 Turbo S ($207,000)

One of the most stunning sports cars ever designed, the Porsche 911 Turbo S is an extortionately priced car in Kristen Stewart’s collection. Packed with a 3.7-liter six-cylinder engine, the 911 Turbo S offers 478 kW of power and prances from 0 to 62 MPH in merely 2.7 seconds, as reported by Porsche. The 2+2 seater embodies the timeless design of the Porsche 911 with a contemporary style. The interior has a newly designed 10.9-inch touchscreen with a Porsche Connect, an intelligent co-pilot that assists with navigation, and a Wet Mode for assistance during wet road surfaces. The head-turning 911 Turbo S costs $207,000.

Chevrolet Suburban ($47,300)

Made for busy households, the Chevrolet Suburban is a family SUV. Equipped with three rows and a comfortable cabin, the Suburban features family-focused safety and technology, costing $47,300. Along with providing a spacious cargo space, it has a mighty V8 engine that produces 355 HP. The interior has a large infotainment display with smartphone integration for Android and Apple users. Chevrolet also offers the Suburban a ten-speed transmission V8 that gives out 420 HP.

Porsche Cayenne ($67,300)

Owned by Stewart in the shade of metallic jet black, Porsche Cayenne is one of the best sports cars for five. The 2017 iteration is a refined version of the Cayenne with a modern interior and agility. It is offered in a 3.0-liter V6 engine and 2.9-liter bi-turbo V6 engine; it cranks out 335 HP and 434 HP, respectively. As mentioned by Auto Express, the infotainment system is occupied mainly by the 12.3-inch LED screen and has its iconic sporting bend inside to offer a lower driving position. The powerful, family-friendly car retails at $67,300.

Toyota T100 ($17,228)

Kristen has always been a fan of Toyotas, and the actress owns not one but two pick-ups in her collection. The Toyota Tacoma was one of the first SUV trucks from Japan that made its debut in the USA in 1992 and cost a whopping $17,228. The brand made the truck look different from the Ford and Chrysler trucks. It was initially equipped with a 3.0-liter V6; however, it later upgraded to a 3.4-liter V6 to produce 190 HP.

Toyota Tacoma ($25,000)

A classic in the garage, Stewart owns a first-generation Toyota Tacoma introduced in the 90s. The Tacoma was offered in three variations: Standard, Extended, and Double Cab. According to Motor Biscuit, Stewart owns a standard cab in red that cost her over $25,000. The Tacoma was manufactured until 2004, and over 150,000 units of the pick-up truck sold every year.

From old pick-up trucks to sleek and fast Porsche cars, Kristen Stewart has a variety of cars in her collection. The star is usually seen driving her pick-up to run errands around Los Angeles. With a sprawling net worth, the actress will add a few more cars to her collection in the next few years. Kristen Stewart is currently making rounds at the awards circuit for her Oscar-nominated performance as Princess Diana in Spencer.
