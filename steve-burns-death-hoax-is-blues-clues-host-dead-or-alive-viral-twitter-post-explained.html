Steve Burns Death Hoax -Is Blues Clues Host Dead Or Alive? Viral Twitter Post Explained

The former Blues Clues host, Steve Burns has completely sealed off his death hoaxes after discussing his exit in the latest viral Twitter post.

Steve Burns is an American multi-talented personality who is actively involved as an actor, musician,  producer, director, as well as a television host.

Besides all the above-mentioned fields, Burns is best known for being the original host of the children’s preschool TV program Blue’s Clues.

The actor worked in that program only for six years before suddenly disappearing from the show. He then never reappeared on the same show that got his Daytime Emmy nomination in 2001.

After the host suddenly left the show and never came back, people began thinking that Burns might have died. What followed was a series of death hoaxes about the actor.

Now that he came back and explained everything through the Twitter clip that has gone viral, people are once again curious to know more details about the man.

Steve Burns Death Hoax- Is He Dead Or Alive? Viral Twitter Post Explained

From the latest viral Twitter Post of the man, it is clear that Steve Burns is still alive and all the talks of his death were only hoaxes.

Death hoaxes of the former Blue’s Clues original host started circulating after he disappeared from the show and was replaced by his brother.

Since viewers couldn’t see him again, they thought that he might have died and the hoax of Burns’ death by accident or drug overdose quickly took over the media.

Now that Steve came back explaining his disappearance through the clip, it is clear that all those speculations were false and Steve Burns is still alive.

In the viral Twitter Post, Steve has revealed that he left the show since he wanted to go to college and continue his studies, as reported by Variety.

The video gets emotional to his fans as he further talks about the incredible support and love he has received from the fanbase.

Following the clip, many viewers have talked about it on the internet as they open up about getting emotional from the video clip.

Steve Burns Wife: Who Is He Married To?

As things stand, it looks like Steve Burns doesn’t have a wife and is yet to get married.

There are no reports of him getting married or having a wife on the internet. Likewise, no reports of him dating someone is available or known to the outsiders as well.

Besides, Burns himself has never revealed any images of being together with someone that looks like his love partner on his social media platforms.

From these facts, we believe that the multi-talented actor and host, Steve Burns is still single.

How Old Is Steve Burns? Age Revealed

The Blue’s Clues original host, Steve Burns’ age is 47 years old.

He was born on October 9, 1973, in Pennsylvania, United States, to his parents Joseph Burns Jr and Janet Burns.

Interested in acting from an early age, Burns was discovered by an agent in his University and he then left school to become a professional actor.

His acting carer started with Blue’s Clues where he auditioned in 1995 thinking of it as another voice-over role but eventually got selected as host, as per his Wikipedia.

What Is Steve Burns Net Worth?

Celebrity Net Worth reports Steve Burns to have an estimated net worth of an incredible $10 million.

It further mentions Burns’ sources of income to be his successful acting career and as the TV host of Blue’s Clues for which he earned massive fame and recognition.

Even after leaving the child show, Steve frequently landed few other roles in different shows and programs.

Does Steve Burns Have Cancer?

Even to this date, no reports or internet articles mentioning Burns to have cancer can be found on the internet.

The only talks of him battling cancer can be found on Twitter feeds that dates around 2015 when the public thought that he either had cancer or died from a drug overdose.
