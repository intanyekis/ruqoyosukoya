Dylan Sprouse Doesnt Know Who He Loves More, Barbara Palvin Or The PS5

Dylan Sprouse and Barbara Palvin share the sweetest relationship, but not for too long!

Unlike his brother, The Suite Life of Zack & Cody actor hasn't been in the limelight all these years. Since his stint on the Disney series ended, Dylan shifted his focus from acting to finishing school and attending college, and he's slowly making a comeback now.

In 2018, Dylan charmed Sports Illustrated model and Victoria's Secret Angel Barbara Palvin, and they've been together ever since! The couple has also moved in together, and live in Brooklyn, New York.

Like many couples across the globe, Dylan Sprouse's unwavering loyalty to Barbara Palvin has been threatened by the arrival of Sony's anticipated Playstation 5. The actor is in such a fix, he doesn't know who he loves more...his girlfriend of two years or the new gaming console.

A Gift For Barbara

Barbara Palvin is a Hungarian model with an incredibly successful career. She was discovered by an agency at just 13 years old, while walking the streets of Budapest with her mother.

She lives in Brooklyn now, but no one has forgotten her back home! Yesterday, Barbara received the greatest gift ever: the PS5, from PlayStation Hungary. The model's fans were surprised at her being a gamer...but even more when Dylan Sprouse shared his excitement about it.

The actor shared a photograph which sees his lady love holding the Playstation 5 (or should we call it his other love?). Barbara was dressed in all-black, and looked radiant!

"Where do I even begin with what this photo does to me lol," Dylan Sprouse captioned the photo.

The model shared an album posing with her new gaming console, and captioned it "Game time." Her fans flooded the comments section, asking her to make use of her new gift before her boyfriend Dylan gets his hands on them!

Dylan And Barbara's Quarantine Series

The couple worked on a quarantine Instagram series titled Breaking Ground, which documents how the couple spend their time amid a pandemic.

From taking quick getaways to building their dream cabin in the woods, the adventurous couple proved that their relationship was stronger than ever, even in trying times.

Barbara and Dylan have released eighteen episodes of the series so far, and hope to continue doing so in the future!
