John Cena, Sheamus and More Appearing at Comic Con

Wizard World has announced that John Cena, Sheamus and The Bella Twins will be appearing at Wizard World Philadelphia in June for photo ops, autographs and Q&A panels. The following is the appearance schedule:

-Cena will be appearing on Sunday, June 5th from 11am until 3pm
-The Bella Twins will be appearing on Sunday, June 5th from 12:30pm until 3pm
-Sheamus will be appearing Friday, June 3rd from 4-7pm
