365 Days: This Day is among the highly-anticipated Netflix releases which just premiered last April 27, 2022. The erotic thriller which premiered on Netflix is a sequel to the film 365 Days released last 2020. The film will show Anna-Maria Sieklucka and Michele Morrone returning as Laura and Massimo who are set to walk down the aisle. Although the trailer shows them getting married, they will have to endure numerous relationship troubles which will be much more complicated after a rival mafia member enters the scene to compete for the love of Laura. Although the film might stir the relationship of Laura and Massimo, they will surely be back stronger as ever and even made them to take their vows to the altar

365 films might have triggered some extremely guilty pleasures for the movie viewers which may have caused its popularity. The film is sort of being compared to the 50 Shades of Grey movies where a woman was kidnapped by a man who then gave her 1 year to fall in love with him. The film is also filled with steamy yet silly sex scenes which did not stop the movie to climb up as one of the most watched film on Netflix. The film however is highly criticized because it was accused of highly contributing and justifying the rape culture. Even the singer Duffy sent Netflix a written letter to object the film. Although they have received quite some backlash, the Netflix execs decided to keep the movie and declined to remove it from their service. The film is comprised of great cast members; take a look at the actors and actresses in film with their corresponding net worth.

Otar Saralidze

Polish actor and dubbing artist Otar Saralidze plays as Domenico in the romantic film. Aside from his role in the 365 Days series, he has done numerous projects throughout his career included the critically acclaimed film Foreign Body in 2014 and The Innocents in 2016. Although he is a Polish actor, he was born in Georgia in January 14, 1990. A lot of fans are wondering whether the actor is married or not, there is rumor going on that he is the husband of his 365 Days co-star Anna-Maria Sieklucka. He currently has a net worth of $650,000.

Kamil Lemieszewski

Polish actor Kamil Lemieszewski playing the role of James Italian Maffia. Starring in the film series 365 Days is not the first time Kamil has been part of a phenomenal movie. He has starred as Jakku Villager in the film Star Wars: The Force Awakens and has become part of the Rogue One: A Star Wars Story as well. He is a familiar to the movie viewers as he had appearances in the Star Wars franchise. He is currently living in the UK while starring in numerous films, theatre plays and commercials. He currently has a net worth of $1.2 million.

Magdalena Lamparska

Magdalena Lamparska plays as the best friend of Laura, Olga, who was believed to be dead when the first film has ended. However she seems to be alive in the second film after seeing the newly-released photos for the film. Other than the 365 Days films, Magdalena has starred in numerous projects including the Polish television series 39 and a Half. She currently has a net worth of $1.5 million as of 2022.

Simone Susinna

Italian actor Simone Susinna plays as Nacho, the rival gangster who is vying for the heart of Laura. He is an addition to the casts to stir the relationship of Laura and Massimo. Simone who was born Catania Italy on November 14, 1993 had his first major breakthrough in the film 365 Days: This day. It was reported that he is a well known model who decided to branch into acting before starring in the film. Simone Susinna currently has a net worth of $1.5 million.

Michele Morrone

Italian actor, model, singer and fashion designer Michele Morrone plays as Don Massimo Torricelli, the love interest of Laura. He is returning to play the character in the film 365 Days: This Day. Don Massimo Torricelli is the leader of the Torricelli mafia family. Aside from his role in the film; he has played numerous roles in several Italian and Polish films such as Netflix: Medici and The Trial. With the success of the 365 film series, he might be able to join numerous Italian celebrities in Hollywood. He currently has a net worth is said to be around $3 million.

Anna-Maria Sieklucka

The Polish actress Anna-Maria Sieklucka plays the female lead role in the film, Laura Biel. Just like Magdalena Lamparska’s character, Laura believes to be dead when the first film of the 365 Days movie ended. She is indeed alive and well at the sequel 365 Days: This Day. Laura and her love interest Massimo is set to be married in the sequel. Anna-Maria Sieklucka born on May 31, 1992 in Lublin, Poland is currently dating the theatre director Łukasz Witt-Michałowski. She currently has a net worth of $5 million.
