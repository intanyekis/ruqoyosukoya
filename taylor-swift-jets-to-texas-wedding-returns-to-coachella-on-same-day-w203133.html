Taylor Swift Jets to Texas Wedding, Returns to Coachella on Same Day

A true jet-setter! Taylor Swift left Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival on Saturday, April 16, to attend a friend’s wedding 1,200 miles away, but she jetted back in time for the night’s festivities.

The pop superstar, 26, posted several pics hanging at the Indio, California, festival with her friends on Saturday morning. She posed by the pool with pals Lorde and Jack Antonoff and took a “family photo” with her whole Coachella crew. But the 1989 songstress had a full weekend schedule and took a break from the festival to support her backup singer Eliotte Nicole at her wedding in San Antonio, Texas.

“GETTING MARRIED TODAY!!!!! #WoodfordPresERved,” the bride wrote on Twitter. 

Swift was spotted mingling with guests and and rocking a white dress with her “bleachella” bleached blonde hair — which she first debuted via the May cover of Vogue — at the ceremony with her mom, Andrea Swift, according to photos from wedding photographer Jake Baine.

After watching her friend tie the knot, the “Shake It Off” singer hustled back to the desert and made it back in time for closing acts (probably thanks to the time difference).

The next day, Swift supported boyfriend Calvin Harris during his Coachella set and fan-girled when Rihanna came out as a surprise guest. “I’ll NEVER forget this moment,” she captioned an Instagram of herself jamming to Rihanna’s 2011 collab with Harris, “We Found Love.”
