Mustafa Rahmi Kocs Net Worth, Biography, Career, Awards, Fact and Life Story

Net Worth:$2.5 Billion

Mustafa Rahmi Koc net worth: Mustafa Rahmi Koc is a Turkish businessman who has a net worth of $2.5 billion. Mustafa Rahmi Koc serves as the honorary president of the family company, Koc Holding. However, he is slowly narrowing his duties, as he handed over the position of Chairman of the Board at Ford Otosan to his younger son Yildirim Ali Koc last year. Among the interests which remain in Mustafa’s possession are those in the Rahmi Koc Museum, Turkey’s first museum dedicated to the history of transportation, industry and communication, which exhibits thousands of objects from gramophone needles to real size ships and planes. Apart from doing extremely well in businesses, he is also known for his love of the sea and luxury yachts. Being the father of three, all of his children Mustafa, Omer and Ali Koc are billionaires, as well as Mustafa Rahmi Koc’s sisters Semahat Arsel and Suna Kýrac.
