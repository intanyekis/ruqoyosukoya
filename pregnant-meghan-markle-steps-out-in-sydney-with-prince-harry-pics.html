Pregnant Meghan Markle Steps Out in Sydney With Prince Harry: Pics

The circle of life! Prince Harry and Duchess Meghan stepped out in Sydney, Australia, on Tuesday, October 16, hours after the palace confirmed they are expecting their first child.

Meghan, 37, stunned in a white sleeveless dress by Karen Gee, while Harry sported a sleek suit during their trip to the Admiralty House with Australia’s Governor General Peter Cosgrove and his wife, Lady Lynne Cosgrove.

A source told Us Weekly that the Duchess of Sussex is “a couple days just shy of her 15-week mark,” noting that she is due around her niece Princess Charlotte’s birthday, May 2.

After attending Princess Eugenie’s wedding at St George’s Chapel at Windsor Castle — the same location they wed in May — on Friday, October 12, the duo jetted off to Australia for their first international tour together. They will also visit Fiji, the Kingdom of Tonga and New Zealand during their tour.
