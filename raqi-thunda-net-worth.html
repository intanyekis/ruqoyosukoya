Raqi Thunda is an American radio disc jockey (DJ) and reality television star with an estimated net worth of $100 thousand. She recently gained notoriety as a cast member of Love and Hip Hop: New York on VH1.

Formerly known as Raquel, she is a popular Music & Pop Culture enthusiast. Born and raised in Miami, she moved to New York where she worked as a corporate finance agent by day. She later became a well-known fierce talking radio personality by night where she appeared in local radio programs, even without invitation sometimes. Her appearances led her to have her own show Hip Hop Nation on the Sirius satellite station. She also hosts a show in the Sunday Night Showcase on Hot 97 in New York and a recurring judge of Freestyle Fridays. She has started a career as an Ambassador DJ.

Raqi Thunda claims that Spinderella from Salt-N-Pepa is her inspiration. He current favorite rappers include A$AP Rocky and Kendrick Lamar.
