The Internet Reacts To Jim Jones Roc Nation Deal

Roc Nation added another heavy hitter and former rival of JAY Z to their growing roster of seasoned rappers. Yesterday, the record label confirmed that Jim Jones has officially joined their team — for management. The new deal surprised fans of both veteran rappers since they had one of the more entertaining beefs in rap history, which still seemed on-going as of 2015.

READ: Jim Jones Says Max B Could’ve Been Like Drake

Jones is on a whole new wave, though. The Harlem native officially ended his beef with Hov at Roc Nation’s office with a simple golden Instagram post. As expected the Internet has a lot to say about this.

A major ‘congratulations’ is owed to Jim Jones for his new business move. It seems as though Jim and Jay have both moved on from the beef, but the Internet apparently has not.
