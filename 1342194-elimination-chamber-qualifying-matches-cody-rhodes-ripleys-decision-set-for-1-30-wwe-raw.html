Elimination Chamber Qualifying Matches, Cody Rhodes, Ripleys Decision Set For 1/30 WWE RAW

WWE is loading up the card for the January 30 episode of .

Hot off the heels of WWE Royal Rumble, Monday’s show will continue the road to WrestleMania in a big way. In tweet, Byron Saxton announced that the United States Championship will be defended in an Elimination Chamber Match at WWE Elimination Chamber on February 18, and the qualifying matches will begin on Monday night. First, Seth Rollins will face Chad Gable. In the second qualifier, Baron Corbin will face Johnny Gargano.

BREAKING NEWS: @ByronSaxton just announced HUGE plans for tonight’s #WWERaw including @CodyRhodes kicking off the show!

The Road to #WrestleMania has begun! pic.twitter.com/UigrFWvtF4

— WWE (@WWE) January 30, 2023

Plus, the two Royal Rumble winners will appear. Cody Rhodes, the men’s winner, will kick off the show, and Rhea Ripley is set to announce her decision as to who she will face at WrestleMania 39.

WrestleZone will have coverage of the show as it airs.
