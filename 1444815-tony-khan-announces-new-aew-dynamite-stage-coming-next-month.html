Tony Khan Announces New AEW Dynamite Stage Coming Next Month

Tony Khan has teased the reveal of a new set that will be revealed soon.

In a recent tweet, Tony Khan shared a poem to celebrate Valentine’s Day, when he revealed that the episode of AEW Dynamite following AEW Revolution will feature a brand new set for the company.

Khan didn’t elaborate on what to expect from the set, other than teasing that AEW won’t be red and blue when the new set is unveiled.

AEW Revolution is set to take place on March 3, 2024, and will be held at the Greensboro Coliseum in North Carolina. The next episode of AEW Dynamite following the pay-per-view will take place on March 6, 2024, when fans will be able to check out the new set.
