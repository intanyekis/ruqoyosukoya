After Two Unsuccessful Marriages, Elaine Callei Trebek Is In A Marital Relationship With Peter Kares

The ex-wife of Alex, Elaine, was born in Columbus, Ohio, the USA. She belongs to the white ethnic background, and her nationality is American. She is a graduate of Ohio State University in journalism. For now, people are curious to know about her marriage in the present day.

Current Husband: Peter Kares

The businesswoman, Elaine, married Peter Kares after divorcing with Alex Trebek. Peter and Elaine exchanged their wedding vows after 19 years of Elaine's divorce, which was on 18 March 2001, and their marriage ceremony was held privately between their close ones.

Previous Marriages And Divorce

Elaine married thrice in her lifetime. Initially, she was in a marital relationship with Louis Callei, with whom she moved to Canada. Whatsoever, she has been very secretive towards her first marriage. From her bonding with Louis, she has a daughter named Nicky. Unfortunately, the two parted their ways in 1974 just like Janette Olsen.

After her first divorce, she got romantically involved with Alex Trebek, who is a prominent American Host and TV personality. They tied the knot on 13 May 1974, the same year she divorced with Louis. The two first met each other at CBC (Canadian Broadcasting Company), where they used to work together.

Like her first marriage, her conjugal relationship with Alex turns out to be a failure as they parted their ways after seven years. To be exact, they officially divorced in 1981. The couple does not share any children, but he became the adoptive father to her daughter, Nicky.
