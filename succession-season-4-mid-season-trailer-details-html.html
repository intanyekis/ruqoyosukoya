Succession Season 4 Mid-Season Trailer: Those Darn Naysayers

Spoilers for Succession season four, episode three: “Connor’s Wedding”

Oh great, Kendall Roy is floating in another body of water. That can’t be good. Succession season four did the damn thing and killed off Logan Roy in episode three. What happens next? A very ugly fight for what’s left of Waystar Royco, of course. In the mid-season trailer, everybody is gunning for Logan’s seat before his ass is even cold. Karl makes a compelling argument for why Tom shouldn’t lead the company, Greg expresses that he is sad by saying “I am sad,” and Shiv gets flirty-flirty drinky-drinky with Lukas Matsson. Everybody has a chance to stab everybody else in the back before the series finale.
