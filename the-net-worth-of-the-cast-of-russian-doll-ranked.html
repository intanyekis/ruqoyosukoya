American comedy-drama television series Russian Doll is a creation of Natasha Lyonne and comedian and actress Amy Poehler which premiered on Netflix in February 2019. The TV series follows the life of Nadia Vulvokov who repeatedly dies in ongoing time loop. The series has received numerous recognition from different award giving bodies which including four Primetime Emmy Award nominations for Outstanding Lead Actress in a Comedy Series and Outstanding Comedy Series. The series was renewed for the second season which recently premiered last April 20, 2022.

The second season of the series will still feature time traveling works of Nadia and Alan which took them both to the past. The series is coming back in its second season with most of the cast from season 1 returning and adding some more casts in the season 2. Take a look at the net worth of each of the casts of Russian Doll season 2.

Ilona McCrea

Actress and writer Ilona McCrea plays the role of Vera Peschauer in the earliest time period shown in the season 2 of Russian Doll. She plays as Nadia’s grandmother who Nadia inhibited during her younger years. She has attempted to steal the valuables of her family during the Nazis in WWII-era Budapest. Ilona McCrea is popularly known for her portrayal as Lia Kratsova in the series Law & Order: SVU. She is still starting her career and currently has about $500,000 estimated net worth.

Carolyn Michelle Smith

American actress Carolyn Michelle Smith plays as Agnes, the grandmother of Alan. Season 2 will show her as the body inhibited by Alan in the year 1962. Agnes will appear in The Void to Alan who knows about time travel more than expected. Carolyn Michelle Smith is popularly known in her portrayal as Charmaine Cole in Viola Davis starrer How to Get Away with Murder. She currently has a net worth of $1 million.

Elizabeth Ashley

American theatre, film and television actress Elizabeth Ashley reprises her role as Ruth Brenner in the year 2022. She portrayed the role of the therapist of Nadia’s mother before Nora’s death. Since Nora’s passing, Ruth has become the maternal figure for Nadia. Season 2 will show Ruth’s health declining while she is struggling with the complications of her smoking. Elizabeth Ashley is popularly known with her portrayal in the TV series such as The Carpetbaggers and Evening Shade. She currently has about $1.3 million of net worth.

Charlie Barnett

American actor Charlie Barnett plays as Alan Zaveri in the Russian Doll. The story of Alan is secondary to Nadia but their life is intrinsically entwined to the main narrative of the series. The season 1 of the series show Alan still struggling to get back to the dating game however when he time traveled, his body was placed in the body of his grandmother. Charlie Barnett is popularly known for his portrayal of Peter Mills on the series Chicago Fire. He currently has a net worth of $1.3 million.

Sandor Funtek

French actor Sandor Funtek plays as Lenny in the season 2 of Russian Doll. He serves as the love interest of Alan’s grandmother, Agnes. He has attempted to escape to the West Berlin. Sandor Funtek’s first lead role was in the series K contrair and he has accumulated a net worth of $3 million.

Annie Murphy

Canadian actress Annie Murphy plays as Ruth Brenner in the present time 1982. When Nadia has travelled back to the year 1982, she has inhabited the body of her mother and came face to face with the young Ruth Brenner which is played by Annie Murphy. Annie Murphy is popularly known for her portrayal of Alexis Rose in the TV series Schitt’s Creek. She currently has a net worth of $4 million.

Natasha Lyonne

American actress, director, writer, and producer Natasha Lyonne plays as Nadia Vulvokov. Natasha reprises her role from the season 1 of Russian Doll where she repeatedly dies in a time loop. The season 1 of the series showed Nadia grow and allow more genuine connections with people. Natasha Lyonne is best known for her portrayal of the role Nicky Nichols in the series Orange is the New Black. She currently has a net worth of $5 million which she accumulated from her modeling and acting career.

Chloë Sevigny

American actress, model and fashion designer Chloë Sevigny plays as Nora, the mother of Nadia, in the series. In the season 1 of the series, Nora was shown as a flashback in the period shortly before her death. Chloe will be reprising her role in the season 2 but will be playing as the younger version of the previous season’s Nora which is about ten days before the birth of Nadia. Chloë Sevigny is popularly known in her portrayal as Shelley in the series American Horror Story. She currently has a net worth of $10 million which she accumulated through modeling and acting.
