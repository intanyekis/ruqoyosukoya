Pumpkin Spice Latte M&amp;M's Are Seriously Real: Details!

OMG for these M&M's! Pumpkin Spice Latte lovers will no longer have to wait in line at Starbucks to get their fix. The M&M's candy is introducing Pumpkin Spice Latte milk chocolate goodness just in time for the fall season.

Mars candy company confirms to Us Weekly that the delicious treats will be available at Target stores at the suggested retail price of $3.19. Buyers will have a chance to snag their own bag (or two, or three!) during the seasonal time frame.

The new bag features a female M&M wearing black glasses and a gray scarf while holding a latte in her hand. It also reveals that the sweet treat will come in autumnal colors such as orange, brown, and white.
