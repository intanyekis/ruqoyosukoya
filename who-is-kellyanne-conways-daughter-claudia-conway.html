Who Is Kellyanne Conways Daughter, Claudia Conway?

The world of politics has been a hot topic, especially as of late! The United States 2020 election had just about all of us talking, however, one person who seems to have had a lot to say is none other than Kellyanne Conway's daughter, Claudia Conway. Kellyanne is mostly known for her role in the Trump campaign and then as a counselor to the president. She has worked extensively with top Republicans as a strategist for the entire GOP. Claudia's mom is not the only one involved in politics, her dad is a corporate attorney who has served many presidents, including Bill Clinton.

This has definitely set the stage for Claudia to follow in either of her parent's footsteps, however, it seems as if she wants to beat to her own drum. The 16-year-old has become an internet sensation after becoming somewhat of a political commentator on TikTok, gaining over 250,000 followers. Conway has since become a public figure who speaks out against her mom and her mom's boss, sparking many of us to wonder who is Claudia Conway and where has she been all this time?

Who Is Claudia Conway?

Kellyanne Conway is certainly one name that you have heard before, particularly if you tune into CNN or FOX News. Conway is mostly known for her work with the Trump administration as a campaigner, strategist, and counselor to the Republican Party. Conway has become one of the most disliked people on Trump's team, and her own daughter, Claudia Conway, seems to also agree with the public's sentiments. Claudia has since become a hot topic after breaking out on TikTok as a political commentary.

While she isn't talking about politics CNN-style, Claudia is telling it like it is! In April 2020, the 16-year-old made her stance on politics very clear, and one person who isn't happy about it, is her mom, Kellyanne. Claudia posts anti-Trump videos on her page where she criticizes the current administration's work, which has caused quite a lot of fury in the Conway household. "Everyone in my family are Trump supporters except for my dad and I", she told the New York Post. "I grew up in a very, very conservative family, so I was only exposed to those views for a very long time", she added.

Things began to click for Claudia in recent years after educating herself on current issues and events, shifting her political beliefs from that of her mothers, to her own! The Jersey-born creator moved to Washington, D.C in 2016, however, the move was less than appealing to Claudia, so much that she created a change.org petition to allow her to stay in New Jersey. As if fans didn't love her enough, Claudia Conway is one of Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez's biggest fans!

Claudia has made it clear that AOC's efforts at changing the system is what has inspired her to want to do the same. "I think that's what I want to do when I'm older, like social justice activism", Claudia said. While Kellyanne Conway may not be as loved, it appears as if Claudia is making it up for the both of them!
