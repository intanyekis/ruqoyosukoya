Mila Kunis Tried Tinder, Grindr for Ashton Kutcher

Risky move, man! Ashton Kutcher must have been pretty confident in his relationship with Mila Kunis, because when they were first dating back in 2012, he asked her to sign up for the matchmaking app Tinder. 

Kunis, 31, told the story to Jimmy Kimmel on Tuesday, Feb. 3, when she stopped by his show to promote her new movie, Jupiter Ascending. Tinder came up, naturally, while the two were chatting about Kutcher's tech investments. 

"People run these things by him all the time. Listen — when we started dating…Tinder hadn't been really out yet," the new mom to baby Wyatt, 4 months, told Kimmel. "And he was like, 'So babe, you know, I got this thing that I'm thinking about…You need to do me a favor and look at this app. Download [it].' I was like, 'No problem!'"

Kunis proceeded to download the app, which lets users like or reject other users within their radius with just a swipe of the finger. 

"I'm on this thing going, 'What is this?!' Like, swiping things left and right," Kunis recalled. "I was like, 'This is amazing!'"

She didn't stop with Tinder, either. "This is stuff that I do at home. He makes me go on all of these websites," the Ted actress quipped of her former That '70s Show costar and love of three years. "What's the gay version of Tinder? Grindr! I've been on Grindr." 

One invention she wasn't crazy about? BellyBeats. "You put the little microphone around the belly, and you can hear the baby's heartbeat," she explained of the device for pregnant women. "I think this is awful. Because unless you know what you're doing, you may not hear the baby's heartbeat, and then a panic ensues. It's like WebMD but bad, it's bad!"

She continued: "If your baby's, like, [at] seven months, you'll hear it anywhere. But if your baby's a little poppy seed, you really have to dig for it…Let me tell you, at three months, I was like, 'Where is it?!'"

Little Wyatt turned out to be fine, of course. Kunis gave birth back in October 2014, and she couldn't be more in love with her little one.
