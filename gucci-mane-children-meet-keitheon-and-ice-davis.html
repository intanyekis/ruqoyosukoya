Gucci Mane Children: Meet Keitheon And Ice Davis: Gucci Mane, officially known as Radric Delantic Davis was born in Bessemer, Alabama, United States to Ralph Everett Dudley and Vicky Jean Davis born on February 12, 1980.

He’s an American rapper and record executive. Gucci Mane started his career at the age of 14 but after his first arrest, he began to take music seriously.

Gucci Mane was arrested after being caught in possession of crack cocaine by an undercover police officer and was sentenced to 90 days in county jail and then probation.
