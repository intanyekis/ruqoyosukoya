Drake Beats Beatles Billboard 100 Record

Drake has done something that no one has been capable of doing for a long time. He managed to claim the golden ticket of having the most top ten singles to reach the U.S. Billboard Hot 100 in a year.

According to Global News, the classic band known as The Beatles could not be beaten in this category since 1964. During their reign, they had 11 Top 10 singles. However, Drake just narrowly beat them by one song recently.

The pat on the back didn't stop there. He even got a text from what he considers his favorite high school teacher.

Now, this was a big achievement but it is safe to assume that everyone kind of knew it was going to happen, if not by him, likely another big-time star. The Beatles could not hold that position since people's tastes have changed over the years, they've also stopped producing songs, which means that they can't compete with the pace of Drake or other modern artists.

While there's a ton of positivity around this, you can't really compare the Beatles and Drake because they're from very different time periods. Drake also has the advantage of streaming, which can enable someone to listen to the same song countless times over again, which could bring his songs up to number one all the time, if enough people did it that is.

This isn't even the first record that Drake has beat. Earlier this year he became the first artist to spend the largest amount of time at the number one spot. That time around, he beat Usher.

No one really knows how he's doing this but it's clear that he is going for more than one spot in the top lists, which means he's going to be remembered for a very long time due to his legendary amount of awards for being a top artist.
