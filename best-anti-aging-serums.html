16 Best Anti-Aging Serums

Finding the Best Anti-Aging Serums

Anti-aging serums can be introduced into your skincare regimen at any age. Whether you are looking to prevent fine lines or want to reduce the appearance of existing wrinkles, serums are the ideal product to not only target aging, but also to improve the overall radiance of skin.

They can be used on their own or along with your favorite moisturizers and creams. Due to how quickly they absorb, serums also work great under makeup, as they have a less greasy texture and don’t leave a residue.

What to Consider When Choosing the Best Anti-Aging Serums

Ingredients

The three most popular ingredients in anti-aging serums are vitamin C, hyaluronic acid and retinol. Vitamin C will boost skin luminosity and make it look less tired, whereas hyaluronic acid will deliver hydration and moisture to make skin appear plumper. Retinol is the most potent ingredient to smooth out lines and wrinkles, but should only be used at night with a SPF 50 applied the following day.

Skin Type 

Those with oily skin should look for non-comedogenic formulas to avoid clogging pores. This will also prevent excessive shine if you prefer to wear your serum under makeup or during the day. Those with dry skin should keep an eye out for serums with hyaluronic acid and vitamin E to keep skin hydrated and fine lines and wrinkles smooth.

Age

For younger skin, using an anti-aging serum on alternating nights is the best method to avoid overloading skin while also taking preventative action against the early signs of aging. For those with mature skin, serum can be used both morning and night for optimum results. Unless it contains retinol, then this should only be applied at night.

Brand

Higher-end brands such as Skinceuticals offer an unrivaled vitamin C serum which is one of the beauty products that is definitely worth the investment. Drugstore brands such as Olay’s super serum also offer an incredible formula with effective ingredients for an affordable price.

Application

After cleansing, serum should be applied by gently massaging in circles across the face. Always apply it before your moisturizer and if possible, leave some time for it to absorb. If applying it in the morning, do it after SPF but before makeup.

What are the Different Types of Best Anti-Aging Serums?

Hyaluronic Acid 

Hyaluronic acid is a powerhouse ingredient which benefits the appearance of skin in numerous ways. Not only does it pump the complexion with moisture and hydration, it also smooths out skin texture and reduces the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

Retinol

Retinol increases collagen production and stimulates new blood vessels for a fresher looking appearance. One of the few ingredients which has scientific evidence to prove it works, while it has amazing results, it can also be quite heavy for skin. In fact, it to be added gradually to your skincare routine until you have built up a tolerance otherwise you may risk unpleasant side effects.

Natural

If you prefer to stay away from harsher ingredients, there are plenty of natural options on the market to help you gently reduce fine lines and wrinkles. Bakuchiol oil is the most popular, as it has similar results to retinol but doesn't yield the same irritation in some shoppers.
