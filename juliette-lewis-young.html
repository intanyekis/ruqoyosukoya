Juliette Lewis Then &amp; Now: Photos Hollywood Life

Juliette Lewis, born June 21, 1973, is one of the 90s most famous faces. Known for playing quirky characters, the star began in Hollywood young, as the daughter of late character actor Geoffrey Lewis. One of her first big roles was in Martin Scorsese’s remake of ‘Cape Fear’. The role earned her an Oscar nom.

After that, Juliette starred in ‘What’s Eating Gilbert Grape’ (1993), ‘Natural Born Killers’ (1994), ‘The Basketball Diaries’ (1995), ‘From Dusk Till Dawn’ (1996), ‘The Other Sister; (1999), ‘Hysterical Blindness’ (2002), ‘Old School’ (2003), ‘Starsky & Hutch’ (2004), ‘Whip It’ (2009), and ‘Ma’ (2019), among others. Her recent roles include the TV series ‘The Firm’ (2012), ‘Wayward Pines’ (2015), ‘Secrets and Lies’ (2015–2016), ‘The Act’ (2019), and ‘Yellowjackets’ (2021–present). Juliette is also musical, as the front-woman of Juliette and the Licks. Since 2009, she has been releasing material as a solo artist.
