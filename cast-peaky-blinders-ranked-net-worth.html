The Cast Of 'Peaky Blinders' Ranked By Net Worth

The cast of Peaky Blinders has one of the most talented lineups of actors and actresses. Netflix picked up the series and made the show into a Netflix original after its growing popularity on BBC. Due to COVID-19, production for the sixth and final season was halted and is now expected sometime during 2022. The anticipation for this season is unlike any other. Fans haven't seen the Shelby's in over two years since season five's release on October 4, 2019.

Why has this series resonated with fans so much? It seems like people don't just like the show, they love it. Cillian Murphy is the star of the show, but he wouldn't be anything without his supporting cast. The Shelby family is willing to do anything for the other and that is why they've made it this far. Villains come and go but the family sticks together no matter what. Let's take a look at the cast of this incredible series and how they've amassed their impressive net worths.

9 Finn Cole: $2 Million Net Worth

Finn Cole may have joined later in the show, but his arrival sure made an impact. Finn plays Michael Gray, the long-lost son of Polly, who ends up joining his cousin's lifestyle in Peaky Blinders. Actress Anya Taylor-Joy plays his wife in the series. Cole has a net worth of $2 million and is best known for his roles in Animal Kingdom, Here Are the Young Men and Dreamland.

8 Paul Anderson: $2 Million Net Worth

The eldest of the Shelby brothers is the unhinged Arthur Shelby. He plays a drunk in the series who does all of Thomas' dirty work. Paul Anderson plays one of the most popular characters on the show and has managed to build his net worth up from it. Anderson landed a major role in The Revenant alongside Leonardo DiCaprio and his Peaky Blinders co-star, Tom Hardy.

7 Sophie Rundle: $2.5 Million Net Worth

Sophie portrays the only Shelby sister in the family, Ada. Whether her brothers respect her opinion or not, she's going to hand it to them! Peaky is definitely Sophie Rundle's breakout role which helped grow her net worth to about $2.5 million. Rundle was also recently in Netflix's The Midnight Sky with George Clooney, Felicity Jones, Tiffany Boone, and Kyle Chandler​​​​​​.

6 Annabelle Wallis: $4 Million Net Worth

Although the love of Thomas' life didn't make it past season three, Grace Shelby deserves to be on this list. The onscreen chemistry between Annabelle Wallis and Cillian Murphy was truly immaculate. It was a shame to see her character go so early on in the series but the show must go on!

5 Aiden Gillen: $8 Million Net Worth

Aiden Gillen plays the part of Aberama Gold who has a dangerous reputation but falls for the one and only Polly Gray. Aberama is a Romany Gypsy hitman-for-hire who does not make it out of season five alive. Gillen was also part of another major television series playing Petyr Baelish, also known as Littlefinger, in Game Of Thrones. It's no wonder this Irish actor has a net worth of $8 million.

4 Sam Claflin: $8 Million Net Worth

Sam Claflin was very late to the game as his character, Oswald Mosley, was only introduced in season five. His character will have a major role in season six as he begins to introduce fascism to Britain. Sam Claflin is a very well-known English actor and has starred in the films, Love, Rosie;Adrift; Me Before You, and of course the legendary Hunger Games films.

3 Cillian Murphy: $20 Million Net Worth

"I don’t pay for suits. My suits are on the house or the house burns down," - Thomas Shelby.

Cillian Murphy is an insanely talented actor who portrays the leader of the Birmingham gang in Peaky Blinders. Thomas Shelby is a master manipulator and entrepreneur who can outsmart anyone who dares to come for him.

Murphy has also appeared in the hit films, Batman Begins,Dunkirk, and Dark Knight Rises, helping to earn his net worth of $20 million. After the show comes to an end next year, he'll begin filming for the Peaky Blinders movie in 2023.

2 Helen McCrory: $25 Million Net Worth

The beloved Helen McCrory passed away from cancer on April 16, 2021. She was an extremely talented actress who gained recognition from her role in the Harry Potter movies as well as her portrayal of Polly Gray in Peaky Blinders. Helen built a name for herself in Hollywood and she will be greatly missed in the film industry. After an incredible career in the acting world, she amassed a net worth of $25 million.

1 Tom Hardy: $45 Million Net Worth

Landing the English actor, Tom Hardy, for the role of Alfie Solomons was huge for the franchise. Hardy is an A-list actor who portrays a secondary character in Peaky Blinder, which just adds to the already stellar main cast. He plays a Jewish gang leader in the series and does it to perfection. Tom Hardy has been in films Venom, Black Hawk Down, Legend, and Mad Max. It is no surprise that Hardy has the highest net worth out of the entire cast.
