Did Taylor Swift Reveal Her BFF Blake Lively's Newest Baby's Name Through Song?

Highlights

  • Taylor Swift revealed Ryan and Blake's third child's name in the song "betty" on her album Folklore.
  • Fans speculate that Daisy May could be the name of Blake and Ryan's fourth child from Taylor's album Midnights.
  • The couple might have chosen a name from songs already released by Taylor Swift to prevent another reveal.

The friendship between Taylor Swift, Blake Lively, and Ryan Reynolds has been the envy of many, though Taylor and Blake's friendship only began due to Instagram shade.

The three became close, and at one point, fans even believed that Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds set Taylor Swift up with her ex-boyfriend Jake Gyllenhaal. The trio is so close that Taylor Swift actually revealed the name of their third child in her song "Betty."

Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds welcomed a fourth child in 2023, but it is unclear if Taylor Swift worked her name into her newer music. However, that has not stopped fans from speculating! After the way Taylor revealed Betty's name, fans went looking for signs of the Reynolds' baby's name on multiple albums.

Taylor Swift Revealed Ryan And Blake's Third Child's Name In "Betty"

Taylor Swift has a habit of working the Reynolds family names into her songs

In 2017, Taylor Swift released her sixth studio album: Reputation. The song "Gorgeous" begins with a baby cooing the title of the track. After the album was released, fans saw James Reynolds, Ryan Reynolds and Blake Lively's eldest daughter, credited as the "baby intro voice" in the production notes.

Following the release of Reputation, Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds had two more children: Inez and Betty. Fans did not know that Betty was the name of their third child until Taylor Swift released her 2020 album Folklore.

The song "betty" features a love story between three people: Betty, James, and Inez. Knowing that James and Inez were the names of the first two children, fans were correct in assuming that Betty was the name of Ryan Reynolds and Blake Lively's third child.

Did Taylor Swift Reveal The Name Of Blake And Ryan's Fourth Child?

Fans think that Taylor's Midnights album might hold a clue to another Reynolds name

In October 2022, Taylor Swift released her tenth studio album Midnights. In the song "You're On Your Own Kid," Taylor Swift sings, "I see the great escape / So long, Daisy May."

This has caused Swifties to speculate that Daisy May might have been the name of Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds' fourth child. Some users believe that the name "Daisy May" fits perfectly with the names of the couple's other three children.

However, other Swifties have speculated that the name refers to the character of Margaret March from the book "Little Women" by Louisa May Alcott. Margaret is known as Meg to her family, but Daisy by her rich friends. It is possible that she combined the name of the first name of the character (Daisy) and the middle name of the author (May) to create a brand new name.

The name Daisy May can also sound like "dismay," matching the melancholy nature of the song.

What Else Could The Name Of Ryan Reynolds And Blake Lively's Fourth Child Be?

Could Ryan Reynolds and Blake Lively have chosen the name from a Taylor Swift song that's already been released?

Since Taylor Swift leaked the name of Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds' third child, it is possible that the A-list couple decided to make sure that she couldn't do it again by choosing a name from a song that Swift had already released.

In 2020, years before the couple's fourth child was born, Taylor Swift released a song called "Dorothea" on her ninth studio album, Evermore. Some Swifties believe that Ryan Reynolds and Blake Lively picked the name of their child from a song that already exists to show their love for Taylor Swift.

Other Swifties believe that Taylor Swift might have made fun of the Reynolds name speculation in her 2024 studio album, The Tortured Poets Department. Although the song "Chloe or Sam or Sophia or Marcus" is about infidelity, other fans have wondered if one of those four names is a clue to the fourth child's name.

Chloe, Sam, Sophia, or Marcus could also be a list of names that Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds were considering for their child but decided not to use. It is also possible that they named their child Clara, as "Clara Bow" is the title of another track on the album.

It is possible that Blake Lively named her daughter Clara Bow after the star of the silent screen, which prompted Taylor Swift to use her as a muse in her own song. There are also songs on the album named "Robin" and "Peter," which might provide a clue to the name of the couple's fourth child.

Although Taylor Swift has yet to share the identity of the individuals who inspired the tracks on The Tortured Poets Department, fans still love to speculate, especially since Taylor Swift called multiple people out in the album.
