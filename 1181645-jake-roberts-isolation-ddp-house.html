Jake Roberts Escapes Isolation, Returning To DDPs House

After a period of uncertainty, Jake Roberts is returning to DDP’s “Recovery Crib.” When Jake Roberts went to AEW’s recent Dynamite taping, DDP made it clear that he wouldn’t be welcome back when he was done. Dallas wanted to ensure that everyone in his home was safe from the virus that’s sweeping the country, and Jake wanted to ensure that AEW’s program with Lance Archer went off without a hitch.

Since that time, Jake has been staying alone in a hotel room, and he’s expressed that it’s been a difficult stretch for him. Because of that, DDP has relented, offering Roberts space back in the home with some conditions. Mainly, he’ll still be in quarantine within the house for the next week or so. He announced this with a tweet featuring Winnie the Pooh cursing, which you can see below.
